GUIMARÃES PARENTE ADVOGADOS is a law firm founded to serve you. It provides from simple to complex non-contentious and contentious legal services to individuals and legal entities. In order to do so, our firm counts on a team of highly qualified people committed to attending to our clients needs and to providing the best legal result to our clients and the Brazilian society. It is a 21th century law firm ready for current challenges. It is modern, efficient, and effective and it is corroborated by the most recent positions held by state, federal, and higher courts and by the jurists of each area.

We provide our services throughout Brazil as well as abroad and in the following languages: Portuguese, English, Spanish and Italian.

Our history

GUIMARÃES PARENTE ADVOGADOS is a dream come true for its founding partner, Fernando Parente, who wanted to found a modern law firm with innovative ideas and differentiated service. Later, the firm gained another partner, Thiago Guimarães, who, driven by the same goal, accepted the challenge of founding a modern law firm that could meet all of our clients needs. Thus, he became part of our team, bringing in a lot of knowledge and rendering high-quality service to our clients.

Since then, GUIMARÃES PARENTE ADVOGADOS has expanded, gained new clients, changed its location and hired interns, lawyers, and associates.
